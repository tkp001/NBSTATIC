It's a me, Mario! I am a bright and cheerful character that is instantly recongnizable with my trademark moustache, I own a beautiful go-kart that I use to race along my favorite tracks.

I live in the Mushroom Kingdom with my brother Luigi. Whenever anyone is in trouble, I always strive to help and be the main character.

I love to collect coins, eat spagetti, jump on goombas and koopas!
